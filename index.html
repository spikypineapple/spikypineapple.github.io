Frequently Asked Questions

How Long Do False Eyelashes Last?

False eyelashes don’t have a set lifespan, however they will last longer if you look after them following each use. The actual amount of time that a pair of lashes will last for is dependent on how often you wear them, which lash glue you use with them, and how carefully you remove them and care for them once you’ve taken them off. You can prolong the life of your false eyelashes by storing them in a lash case (to retain their shape and prevent them from being crushed in your makeup bag or box.) You can also remove them gently after use to prevent the band degrading or falling apart (which will cause the lashes to shed and become unusable.) Finally, you can prolong the life of your lashes by cleaning them once removed – by carefully removing any residual glue left on the lash band.
